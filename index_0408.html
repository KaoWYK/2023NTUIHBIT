2023臺灣大學國際手球邀請賽

National Taiwan University International Handball Invitational Tournament

關於我們
About Us


本賽會起源於民國77年6月本校所舉辦的「北區大專院校手球邀請賽」，隔年即擴大邀請全國大專院校參賽，更名為「臺灣大學手球邀請賽」。 而因應國際化趨勢，擴展大學生的國際視野與促進身心健康，培養未來優秀領導菁英人才，從86年開始邀請國外大學參賽，同時更名為「沈赫哲盃臺灣大學手球邀請賽」。 至93年更改為每兩年舉辦一次，於95年定名為「臺灣大學國際手球邀請賽」。

本賽會設有學生組及校友組，提供學生極佳之國際交流學習機會，且為在校生與畢業校友學習聯絡的園地。 參加的大學，主要來自日本、新加坡、中國、香港、澳門。近年也提供名額給俱樂部球隊參賽，如在新加坡和上海工作的歐洲人亦曾組隊來參加校友組的比賽。

因新冠肺炎疫情趨緩，各國陸續開放交流，為能持續與各國大學手球同好交流，本賽事將於今年暑期恢復舉辦。憶起2018年有來自6個國家及地區， 24所知名大專院校、俱樂部球隊以及宏都拉斯國家隊，共44支隊伍近800位好手參賽，賽會熱鬧精彩，期盼能邀請您的大學（俱樂部）來臺北參加比賽，並遊覽美麗的寶島。


主辦單位：國立臺灣大學、 臺北市政府體育局、 中華民國手球協會、台北市體育總會手球協會

協辦單位： 中華民國大專院校體育總會手球委員會

通訊地址：臺北市10617大安區羅斯福路四段一號。臺大體育室




The tournament began with the “North Area University Handball Invitational Tournament” in June 1988. A year later, we expanded the tournament, renamed as "National Taiwan University Handball Invitational Tournament", and invited all universities in Taiwan to participate in it. In line with the trend of globalization, we hoped to cultivate outstanding future leaders by expanding students’ international perspective. Therefore, in 1997, we started to invite overseas universities to the tournament and change its name to “Shen He Zhe Cup National Taiwan University Handball Invitational Tournament.” Since 2004, the tournament is held every two years instead. In 2006, the tournament was officially named “National Taiwan University International Handball Invitational Tournament.”

In this tournament, we categorize participants into Student Team and Alumni Team. We provide excellent international exchange opportunities for students. It is also a good chance for students and alumni to communicate and learn from each other. The participating universities are mainly from Japan, Singapore, China, Hong Kong and Macao. In recent years, we also invite club teams to join us. For example, a club formed by Europeans in Singapore and Shanghai once participated in the Alumni Team.

National Taiwan University International Handball Invitational Tournament is held every two years. In the last few years, Asian university handball teams and clubs are the main attenders. All games in the tournament are organized in the air conditioning indoor sport court. We cordially invite you to attend 2023 National Taiwan University International Handball Invitational Tournament in Taipei and enjoy the beautiful view in Taiwan.


The Organizer: National Taiwan University, Department of Sports, Taipei City Government, Chinese Taipei Handball Association, Taipei Handball Association

The Co-organizer: Chinese Taipei University Sports Federation Handball Committee

Address: No. 1, Sec. 4, Roosevelt Rd., Taipei 10617, Taiwan (R.O.C.), Department of Athletics NTU

Meal

There are several restaurants in our campus. For example, 9th Women's Dorm Restaurant and Xiao-Fu Food Services. There are also many restaurants and shops in Lu Ming Square. Most of them are cheaper than the restaurants outside the campus.

Besides them, there are also many unique restaurants on the Xinsheng South Road. Shi-Da Night Market and Gongguan Commercial District are 12-15 minute walk from NTU Sports Center.


Traffic

- Bus: one-zone fare or two-zone fare; each zone-fare is NT$15

- MRT: NT$20 for basic fare; Taipei MRT Fare Chart; NTU Sports Center is close to G07 Taipower Building Station or G08 Gongguan Station

- Taxi: NT$70 for the first 1.25 km; NT$5 for extra additional 200m; NT$5 for every 80 seconds at a speed under 5km/h; The late night surcharge remains at NT$20 from 23:00 to 06:00 for each journey.
